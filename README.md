# Number Guessing Game (CLI)

A feature-rich, command-line interface (CLI) number guessing game built with Python. Test your luck, manage your attempts under different difficulty levels, track your high scores, and get dynamic clues when you are stuck.

## Features

- **Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Choose between Easy (10 chances), Medium (5 chances), or Hard (3 chances).
- **Dynamic Hint System**: Stuck on a number? Type `hint` after your first guess to get clues like whether the number is even/odd or how close you are (e.g., "Burning hot!").
- **Live Performance Timer**: Tracks exactly how many seconds it takes you to solve the puzzle or survive.
- **Persistent Session High Scores**: Tracks and updates your fewest attempts required to win, categorized by difficulty level.
- **Replayability**: Play as many rounds as you want without losing your current high score records.
- **Robust Validation**: Gracefully handles typos, letters, and out-of-bounds numbers without crashing.

## Requirements

- Python 3.6 or higher
- No external libraries required (uses built-in `random` and `time` modules)
